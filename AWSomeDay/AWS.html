What is UAD

University AWSome Day is an important event organized by AWS in wellknown universities around the world. It is a great opportunity for AWS to find talents, and also the opportunity for students to learn more from professional.

UAD tailored to the technology talents and university students who has intersted to cloud computing and machine learning, this event is a great opportunity to learn more about the AWS cloud and face-to-face with AWS experts. For the training courses designed for Amazon Web Services (AWS) Cloud Services, our professional instructors will lead you through the core services of AWS, including: Compute, Storage, Database, and Networking. In this course, you can not only discuss the problem face to face with the instructor, after the course, you may understand how to build a secure and highly scalable Scalability information service platform on the AWS platform. To create more success for your research and work.
