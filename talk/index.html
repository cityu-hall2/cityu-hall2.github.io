SPECIAL Hall Night Talks

Presented by the Student Residence Office (SRO), HSBC Prosperity Hall (Hall 2), and supported by Jockey Club Humanity Hall (Hall 1), Alumni Civility Hall (Hall 3), Jockey Club Academy Hall (Hall 4), Chan Sui Kau Hall (Hall 5), Lee Shau Kee Hall (Hall 6), Jockey Club Harmony Hall (Hall 7), Yip Yuen Yuk Hing Hall (Hall 8) and CityU Apps Lab , SPECIAL Hall Night Talks (業昕之夜) are special monthly talks providing a dynamic and quality learning platform to student residents to meet and talk with renowned elites in the society. Guests will share with you their Keys to Success on the SPECIAL areas of:

S - Serve the world
P - Passion
E - Entrepreneurship
C - Coding
I - Innovation
A - Aspiration
L - Life and career
