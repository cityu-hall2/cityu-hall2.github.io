About Event

CityHack is back! CityHack 2022 is a 24-hour Hackathon organized by CityU students. This year, we have four categories with specific problems. We welcome all students from CityU or other local universities to work together and develop innovative products to address the given problems using cutting-edge technologies.

FAQ
1. I'm not a CityU student, Can I register?

Sure. We welcome all Hong Kong undergraduate and postgraduate students to register for this contest.

2.Do I have to stay in the place for the whole time from 9 am 29th to 7 pm 30th? I have another competition to participate in.

Ideally you are expected to be present and participate throughout the weekend. But it is acceptable to work remotely. However, you definitely have to join the presentation and award ceremony from 3:20pm to 7:00pm on 30th.

3. Do I have to form a group? May I work individual or find a group from the place?

It is preferred to form a group, however, it is optional. If you registered individually and are in need of a group, the discord group can assist you to form one. However, you are advised to form a group before the hackathon.

4. What is the topic and expectation for this competition?

The topics would be related to AI, Biotech, Edutech and Fintech, but the detailed information will be announced during the opening ceremony. The expected outcome of the competition would be a product (e.g. Website, Mobile App) or a Solution (e.g. a predictive model, a pipeline) to the problem. There will be technical staff on stage and mentors in the discord channel during the hackathon event to answer your questions regarding any technical issues.

5. If I form a group, do they have to be undergraduate CityU students or can they be post-graduate students or a student from a different university?

There are no strict limitations on the identity of the contestants unless he/she is the organizer/sponsor of this event. In fact, many registered contestants are not from CityU. But each team should include at least one CityU Student.

6. May I present and develop something I already have?

No. Your submitted project must be built during the CityHack hackathon.
